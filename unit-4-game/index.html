Crystal's Collector

Game Rules

  1. A random number will be given to you at the start of the game."Random Number"
  2. There are four crystals below:
    • blue
    • green
    • red
    • yellow
  3. By clicking on a crystal, a specific amount of points will be added to your total score.
  4. The value of each crystal is hidden until you first click it.
  5. You win the game by succefsully matching your total score to the random number.
  6. You lose the game if your total score goes beyond the random number.
  7. Each time you win/lose or reset the game the values of the crystals will change.
